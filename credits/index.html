How credits work

  • 1 credit = 1 reply in most cases.
  • If any request costs more than 1 credit, we’ll tell you first and ask you to confirm.
  • You’ll always see: “You have X credits. Reply YES to use one.”

We use the word “credits” to mean access to digital replies. Credits are not money or loans.

What can I get with a credit?

We’re growing the list over time. If something needs extra credits, we’ll ask before using them.
