SMS Opt-In Policy 📱

Welcome to 740-TEXT-JOE a friendly place where you can receive cartoons, jokes, photos, or fun texts that make your day a little better. This page explains how you opt in (and out) of messages from us.

How You Opt In

You may choose to receive messages by:

  • Texting one of our official numbers
  • Replying YES after being offered a gift or free credit
  • Joining a campaign or promotion that clearly mentions SMS

What Kinds of Messages?

You may receive:

  • Personalized cartoon photos or messages
  • Redemption or confirmation replies
  • Friendly check-ins or replies based on your message

How to Opt Out

You can opt out at any time by replying with one of the following:

  • STOP
  • UNSUBSCRIBE
  • CANCEL

You'll get a confirmation and we won’t message you again unless you restart the conversation.

If you’re ever having a rough day, text us something fun and we’ll try to make you smile.

Your Privacy Matters

We don't sell or share your personal info ever. Limited message logs may be stored only to make sure our service works correctly and to help resolve any issues.

This policy might evolve as we grow, but we'll always prioritize your trust and experience.
