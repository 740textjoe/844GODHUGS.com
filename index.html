What is GODHUGS?

GODHUGS is a message-based experience delivering joy, comfort, and custom cartoon hugs via SMS. No spam. Just kindness, cartoons, and gentle encouragement.

Text us a photo and find out. Or just send a message like HUG, HUG ME, or HUGS. We’ll use one credit to reply with a personalized image, cartoon, or spiritual message. Want to surprise a loved one? You can also REDEEM a gift code or purchase credits to send joy to someone else.
